chili

Scientific Name:

Capsicum annuum

Family:

Solanaceae

Best Time to Grow:

Winter

Description:

Chili pepper, any of several species and cultivars of very hot, pungent peppers in the nightshade family (Solanaceae). Chili peppers are native to the Americas and are cultivated in warm climates around the world.
